Kehlani Over the Years

Kehlani Ashley Parish also known as Kehlani is a grammy nominated artist. She is most associated to stars such as Nick Cannon, Destin Conrad, Megan Thee Stallion and other R&B artists. Since 2014, her sound has evolved and audience has grown gloabally. Kehlani, mostly known for R&B has experimented with her sound expecially through Blue Water Road and Crash. She has referenced the 90s hip hop sound and also has included Rock & Roll and country. Recently, she has dropped two singles titled "Folded" and "Out The Window" which has been viral on social media. Folded has been nominated for multiple Grammy's including Best R&B Song. An announcement for an upcoming album is highly anticipated since the release of the Grammy nominated song over the summer.
